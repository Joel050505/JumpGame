Game Features

Coin Collection

Collect coins throughout your journey to unlock new abilities, character customizations, and gain access to an exclusive city.

Exciting Challenges

Test your skills with diverse challenges designed to push your limits. Complete time trials, precision runs, and special missions to earn rewards.

Level Progression

Gain experience and level up Willie's abilities. Making him happier, stronger, and more agile. Unlock new characters and levels, and overcome increasingly difficult obstacles.

Day & Night Modes

Experience the game in two distinct atmospheres. Switch between day and night modes to discover different mechanics, and collectibles that transform the gameplay experience.
